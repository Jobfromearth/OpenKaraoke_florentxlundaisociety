# 🎬 Elevator Pitch Script — SingAlong AI
**Event:** Florent × Lund AI Society Hackathon
**Format:** Talking Head (0:00–0:20) → Screen Demo (0:20–2:20) → Talking Head (2:20–3:00)
**Language:** English
**Total runtime:** ~2:45–3:00

---

## PART 1 — HOOK (0:00–0:20) | On Camera

> "You're watching your favorite anime. The opening theme hits — you love it. You want to sing along.
>
> But it's Japanese. And you have no idea how a single word sounds.
>
> You Google the lyrics. You find them. But reading Japanese script? That's a different skill entirely.
>
> This moment — loving a song but being locked out of it — happens to hundreds of millions of people, every day."

---

## PART 2 — THE PROBLEM (0:20–0:45) | On Camera

> "Current solutions don't work. Romanization systems like Romaji or Pinyin require you to *learn the system first*. Google Translate gives you meaning — not pronunciation. Karaoke apps assume you already know how the language sounds.
>
> There's no tool that meets you where you are — and lets you sing *right now*, in a language you've never studied."

---

## PART 3 — SOLUTION DEMO (0:45–2:10) | Screen Recording of App

**[Show: landing page → search bar]**

> "We built SingAlong AI. Here's how it works."

**[Type "Gurenge" or any anime/pop song into search bar]**

> "You search for any song."

**[Select a YouTube result]**

> "You pick a YouTube video."

**[Show phonetics loading, then appearing line by line]**

> "In seconds, our AI generates singable phonetic transliterations — mapped word by word, color coded so you can follow along visually."

**[Press play, show lyrics scrolling in sync]**

> "The lyrics scroll in real time, synced to the video. You don't need to know the language. You just... sing."

**[Click a line to enter loop mode, show segment scanning left-to-right]**

> "Struggling with one line? Click it. It loops — and each syllable lights up as it plays, so you can drill it until you've got it."

**[Show phonetic toggle on/off]**

> "And once you're confident, you can turn off the phonetics and perform the real lyrics."

---

## PART 4 — TECHNICAL APPROACH (2:10–2:30) | On Camera

> "Under the hood: we use **Claude AI** to generate phonetics that are *singable*, not just readable — it understands rhythm and syllable mapping. Lyrics come from an open-source database. Everything runs on **Next.js** with a **YouTube embed**, and phonetics are **cached after first generation** — so the more people use it, the faster it gets.
>
> The architecture is language-agnostic. We launched with Mandarin phonetics for Chinese speakers — 1.4 billion potential users. But the same pipeline works for any target language."

---

## PART 5 — CLOSE (2:30–3:00) | On Camera

> "Music is one of the most universal human experiences. But language has always been a wall between you and the songs you love.
>
> We used AI to tear that wall down.
>
> We built this in 12 hours. Imagine what comes next."
